Understanding Heart Health: Prevention and Care

October 15, 2025 By Dr. Soje Michael Osisiogu 1,250 Views

Heart disease remains one of the leading causes of mortality worldwide, but with proper prevention and care, many risks can be mitigated. At Heirs Multispecialist Hospital, our cardiology team is dedicated to providing comprehensive heart health services.

Key Prevention Strategies

  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Engage in regular physical activity, aiming for at least 150 minutes per week.
  • Monitor blood pressure and cholesterol levels regularly.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption.

Our specialists recommend annual check-ups to catch potential issues early. With advanced diagnostics available at our facilities in Oye Ekiti and Ado Ekiti, we ensure personalized care plans for every patient.

Treatment Options

From medication management to minimally invasive procedures, our team uses cutting-edge technology to treat various heart conditions. We focus on patient education to empower individuals in their health journey.
