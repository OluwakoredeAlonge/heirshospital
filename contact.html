Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about appointments and visits

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by filling out the form above, calling our hotline at +234 123 456 789, or visiting either of our branches in person.

How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond to appointment requests within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our emergency hotline.

Do you accept health insurance?

Yes, we are NHIS accredited and accept various health insurance plans. Please bring your insurance card to your appointment.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, please contact us at least 24 hours in advance to cancel or reschedule. Call +234 123 456 789 or email info@heirsmultispecialist.com.

What if I have an emergency?

For medical emergencies, call our 24/7 emergency hotline at +234 123 456 789 or visit our emergency department at the Oye Ekiti main branch immediately.
