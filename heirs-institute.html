Heirs Institute of Allied Health

A subsidiary of Heirs Multispecialist Hospital, committed to addressing the critical shortage of skilled allied health professionals in Nigeria through comprehensive, accredited training programs.

Building Nigeria's Healthcare Future

With over a decade of experience providing high-quality healthcare in Ekiti State, we recognize the urgent need for comprehensive training programs to bolster the healthcare workforce, particularly in the private sector. Our institute offers cutting-edge training designed to produce competent, certified professionals ready for immediate employment.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, accessible training in allied health professions, bridging Nigeria's healthcare workforce gap through innovative education and practical experience that meets international standards.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's premier institute for allied health education, producing competent professionals who elevate healthcare standards and contribute to ending medical tourism.

Our Values

Excellence in education, integrity in practice, compassion in care, innovation in learning, and commitment to producing healthcare professionals who serve with dignity and competence.

Accredited Training Programs

NBTE-accredited programs designed to provide hands-on training and certification in key allied health fields

Community Health Extension Worker (CHEW)

Comprehensive training in primary healthcare delivery, disease prevention, and community health education.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • NBTE Accredited
  • Clinical Placement

Pharmacy Technician

Learn pharmaceutical compounding, dispensing, inventory management, and patient counseling.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • PCN Certification
  • Hands-on Training

Medical Laboratory Technician

Master diagnostic testing, specimen collection, laboratory equipment operation, and quality control.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • MLSCN Registration
  • Modern Lab Access

Health Information Management

Train in medical records management, health data analysis, coding, and healthcare informatics.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • NBTE Certified
  • Digital Systems

Radiography Technician

Learn X-ray imaging, CT scans, radiation safety, and diagnostic imaging techniques.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • RRBN Registration
  • State-of-art Equipment

Dental Technology

Master dental prosthetics fabrication, orthodontic appliances, and dental laboratory procedures.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • NBTE Accredited
  • Modern Lab

Physiotherapy Assistant

Training in therapeutic exercises, patient rehabilitation, and physical therapy techniques.

  • Duration: 18 Months
  • Clinical Practice
  • Practical Focus

Environmental Health Technology

Learn public health inspection, sanitation, waste management, and environmental safety.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • NBTE Certified
  • Field Work

Admission Requirements & Process

Simple steps to begin your healthcare career

Entry Requirements

Academic Qualifications

  • Minimum of SSCE/WAEC/NECO with 5 credits including English and Mathematics
  • Science subjects required for specific programs
  • Age: Minimum 16 years at entry

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Original and photocopies of credentials
  • Birth certificate or age declaration
  • Passport photographs (4 copies)
  • Medical fitness certificate

Application Process

1

Submit Application

Complete and submit the online application form with all required documents.

2

Entrance Examination

Take the institute's entrance examination (English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge).

3

Interview & Screening

Attend an interview and document verification session with our admissions team.

4

Acceptance & Enrollment

Receive admission letter, pay fees, and complete enrollment to begin your program.

Important Dates

Application Period: Ongoing admissions throughout the year

Entrance Exams: Conducted monthly

Program Start: Multiple intakes - January, May, and September

Scholarship & Financial Aid

Making quality healthcare education accessible to all

Merit Scholarships

Up to 50% tuition coverage for outstanding academic performance

  • Entrance exam score above 85%
  • 5 distinctions in SSCE/WAEC
  • Renewable annually based on performance

Need-Based Aid

Financial assistance for deserving students with demonstrated need

  • Up to 40% tuition support
  • Payment plan options available
  • Work-study opportunities

How to Apply for Scholarships

Submit your scholarship application along with your admission application. Include:

Personal statement explaining need or merit

Two recommendation letters

Supporting financial documents (if need-based)
