About Heirs Multispecialist Hospital

A world-class specialist hospital set to end medical tourism in Nigeria by offering high-quality medical services comparable to what is obtainable in leading hospitals in Nigeria and beyond.

Excellence in Healthcare

Operating from our two branches in Oye-Ekiti and Ado-Ekiti, we provide state-of-the-art medical facilities in a serene environment, committed to delivering exceptional patient care across all specialties.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, patient-centered healthcare that combines medical excellence with genuine compassion, ensuring every individual receives personalized care tailored to their unique needs.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in our region, recognized for innovative treatments, exceptional outcomes, and our unwavering commitment to ending medical tourism in Nigeria.

Our Promise

Every patient will receive the highest quality care in a comfortable, supportive environment where their health, dignity, and well-being are our top priorities.

Our Journey

Over a decade of excellence in healthcare delivery

2010 - Foundation

Heirs Multispecialist Hospital was established in Oye-Ekiti with a vision to provide world-class healthcare services to Ekiti State and beyond.

2015 - Expansion to Ado-Ekiti

Opened our second branch in Ado-Ekiti at Plot 3, Orimolade Crescent, Ilokun Estate, bringing our services closer to more communities.

2020 - Heirs Institute Launch

Established Heirs Institute of Allied Health to address the critical shortage of skilled allied health professionals in Nigeria, committed to training the next generation of healthcare workers.

2024 - Continued Excellence

Celebrated over a decade of service with advanced medical equipment and expanded specialties, maintaining our commitment to ending medical tourism in Nigeria.
