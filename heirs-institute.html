Empowering Healthcare Professionals

Heirs Institute of Allied Health

A subsidiary of Heirs Multispecialist Hospital, committed to addressing the critical shortage of skilled allied health professionals in Nigeria. With over a decade of experience providing high-quality healthcare in Ekiti State, we recognize the urgent need for comprehensive training programs to bolster the healthcare workforce, particularly in the private sector.

Training Excellence Since 2025

Our institute offers cutting-edge training programs designed to equip students with practical skills and knowledge for successful careers in allied health. We focus on hands-on learning, industry partnerships, and certification preparation.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, accessible training in allied health professions, bridging the gap in Nigeria's healthcare workforce through innovative education and practical experience.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's leading institute for allied health education, producing competent professionals who elevate healthcare standards across the nation.

Our Promise

Every student will receive comprehensive training, mentorship, and support to launch a successful career in healthcare, with a focus on excellence and compassion.
